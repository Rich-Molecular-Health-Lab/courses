Exam Policy

All course exams follow a traditional, in-person, synchronous format.

Most of the course schedule may change significantly as we progress. As a result, the content covered in each exam may change accordingly, but I will keep to the exam dates and times that I set at the beginning of the semester.

You should mark those dates and times on your calendar now, because I will only offer makeup opportunities under extenuating circumstances that the Accessibility Services Center communicates directly to me.
